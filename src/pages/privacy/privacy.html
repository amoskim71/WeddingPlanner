Privacy Information

Wedding Planner saves all of your information on your phone. None of it gets sent back to us or any ad company.

Your search query and location that you input in Explore gets sent through Foursquare to retrieve the results. However this information is not connected to you as a user in any way.

If you don't agree with any of this, this app may not be for you. If this sounds alright, press OK.
